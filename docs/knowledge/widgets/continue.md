# Continue Widget

The Continue widget helps you pick up where you left off. It shows your most recent work session and allows you to quickly resume.

## What It Shows

- Last workspace you were in
- Recent notes you edited
- Quick resume options

## How to Use

- Click to continue your last session
- Use chat: "open continue"
- Automatically tracks your work progress
