# Recent Widget

The Recent widget shows your most recently opened items in this entry. It provides quick access to workspaces and notes you've been working with.

## What It Shows

- Recently opened workspaces
- Recently accessed notes
- Items sorted by last access time

## How to Use

- Click an item to open it
- Use chat: "open recent" or "show recent"
- Items update automatically as you navigate
