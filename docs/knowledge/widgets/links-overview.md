# Links Overview Widget

Links Overview shows all your link categories at a glance. It provides a bird's-eye view of your Quick Links panels and their contents.

## What It Shows

- All Quick Links panels (A, B, C, D, E)
- Link counts per panel
- Quick navigation to any panel

## How to Use

- Use chat: "open links overview"
- Click a category to open that Quick Links panel
- See totals and organization at a glance
