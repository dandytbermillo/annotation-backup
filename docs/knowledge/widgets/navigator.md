# Navigator Widget

The Navigator lets you browse your folder structure and organization tree. It shows the Knowledge Base hierarchy where all your notes and folders are organized.

## What It Shows

- Knowledge Base folder tree
- Subfolders and their contents
- Notes organized by location

## How to Use

- Expand folders to see contents
- Click items to open them
- Use chat: "open navigator"
- Create new folders and notes from here
