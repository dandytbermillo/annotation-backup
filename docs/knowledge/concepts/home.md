# Home

Home is your main entry dashboard. It serves as the central hub where you can access all your widgets, quick links, and navigate to different workspaces.

## What You Can Do

- View and interact with dashboard widgets
- Access Quick Links for fast navigation
- Open workspaces to work with your notes
- See recent items and continue where you left off

## Key Features

- **Widgets**: Customizable panels showing different information
- **Quick Links**: Shortcuts to frequently accessed items
- **Navigation**: Jump to any workspace or note from here
